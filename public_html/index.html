The meaning of TCG: A trading card game among other names, is a type of card game that mixes strategic deck building elements with features of trading cards, origins date back to 1993 when Magic first introduced itself to the world.Generally a player may begin playing a CCG with a pre-made starter deck, and then customize their deck with a random assortment of cards acquired through booster packs, or from trading with other players, building up their own library of cards.[3] As a player obtains more cards, they may create new decks from scratch from their library. Players are challenged to construct a deck within limits set by the CCG's rules that will allow them to outlast decks constructed by other players.
